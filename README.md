## Fitness Tracker 🏋️‍♂️

Hey, I built this project called **Pulse** — it’s basically a simple fitness + health dashboard that runs completely in your browser.

I made this because I got tired of switching between apps for workouts, calories, and water tracking… and most of them either need signups or push subscriptions. I just wanted something fast and private that *just works*.

So yeah, this is my attempt at that.

Everything you enter is saved using localStorage, so your data stays on your device and doesn’t go anywhere.

## What it does

**Dashboard**  
Shows a quick overview of your day — stats, recovery score, and how intense your day was based on what you actually did.

**Workout Planner**  
You can add workouts, set intensity, and mark them as done.

**Nutrition Tracker**  
Log meals and keep track of calories, protein, carbs, and fats.

**Water Tracking**  
Simple buttons to log water (because typing every time is annoying).

**Goals & Progress**  
Track things like weight, sleep, steps, and even mood weekly.

**Extra Tools**  
Includes BMI and BMR calculators, plus a small helper to estimate calories for cutting or bulking.

## How to run it

No setup needed at all:

1. Download or clone the repo  
2. Open `index.html` in your browser  
3. Done

If the dashboard looks empty, there’s a **“Load Demo”** button in the sidebar — it fills in some sample data so you can see how everything works.

## Tech used

**HTML + CSS**  
Used flexbox/grid and tried to keep the UI clean and modern

**Vanilla JavaScript**  
No frameworks — just handling everything manually (state, calculations, DOM updates)

**localStorage**  
So your data stays saved even after refresh
